About Us

At Nest Guard Property Management LLC, we believe property management should be about more than just collecting rent and fixing leaky faucets—it’s about protecting your investment and creating lasting peace of mind.
Founded in Portland, Oregon, NestGuard was built on a simple promise: to care for every property as if it were our own. We combine local expertise, hands-on management, and real human service to make sure your rental experience is stress-free.

What makes us different?

  • No runaround. You’ll always reach a real person when you need us.
  • Full-service management. From tenant screening and leasing to maintenance and rent collection, we’ve got every detail covered.
  • Trust & transparency. Clear communication and honest reporting keep you in control of your investment.


Whether you own a single rental home or a growing portfolio, NestGuard is here to guard what matters most—your property, your time, and your peace of mind.
